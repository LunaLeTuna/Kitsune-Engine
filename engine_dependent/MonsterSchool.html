Quest lines

  • Great Escape
  • Beat the boss of the school, the principal, and exit the school. Then make your way home for lunch. When you got home your mom was expecting you. “Again?” she said. “yeah”
  • New adventure - the best ending
  • Instead of going out of detention, if you wait 10 minutes you are released to 4th  hour history class.
  • The character learns in history class that there is a world above the surface and the reason they are underground was that they were once humans but the lab exploded releasing living goo that transformed them into monsters. by a village 37 years ago.They were banished to the underground because everyone thought they were too dangerous to just let wandering around. So during  recess, you gather some kids start to battle out of school. With his friends he met along the way, they fight their way through the town’s police, the black forest, and found the exit to the surface… The End?
  • Basement’s lab
  • The basement of the school, there is a lab where you can see screens of all the classrooms, odd poisons, broken lab tubes, a teacher that is charging(they are a robot), and ect…
  • Most of the parents don’t have activated powers, yet for some reason they are still hard to beat, but the students have their powers activated. The food is the reason for this revealed in the lab, and why it tastes so bad… Problem is this can also limit the life span of the kids in the school. So you and your party go and try to start an uprising.
  • Smelly escape
  • You enter the sewer through on of the toilets
  • You escape the school through the sewers but there are evil monsters that you must fight through. But you eventually get overwhelmed and get knocked out. You wake up and there is a huge blob with a crown, the underground of the underground king(glopers). *It stares at you blankly with it’s two huge googly eyes for eyes* and goes into a vent that it is sitting on.
  • Prison escape (not 100% sure this is going in)
  • There is a 2.5% chance to get arrested at the end of floor 5
  • You are about to go down the stairs to floor 4, then police surround you. Your party runs the other direction and more cops appear. Then you get tazed from behind. You wake up in a cell, complete with concrete floor, walls, roof, and bed.
  • Goo Attack
  • Free DLC for the game for halloween
  • You, the only kid who brings his lunch to school, have everyone against you. All the kids in the school have turned into beasts. All so, the power goes out in most parts in the school… because halloween
